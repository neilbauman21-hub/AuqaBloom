AI Plant Detection

Computer vision analyzes every plant in real-time, detecting growth patterns, nutrient deficiencies, and potential diseases before they become problems. Our AI learns your specific crops and optimizes conditions automatically.

Smart Water Control

Each box independently manages water levels, pH balance, and circulation. The AI predicts plant needs based on growth stage, weather, and historical data—eliminating waste and ensuring perfect hydration 24/7.

Dynamic Lighting

Full-spectrum LED systems adjust intensity, color temperature, and photoperiod automatically. The AI optimizes light recipes for each growth stage, maximizing photosynthesis while minimizing energy costs.

Precision Nutrients

Automated nutrient dosing systems deliver exact formulations to each box. The AI monitors EC levels, adjusts feeding schedules, and prevents deficiencies—turning your farm into a set-it-and-forget-it operation.

Predictive Analytics

Machine learning models forecast yields, optimize harvest timing, and predict maintenance needs. Get alerts on your phone, not problems in your farm. Focus on scaling your business, not babysitting plants.

Zero Pest Interference

Worry about the filthy animals, not your awesome plants! Sealed, climate-controlled environments eliminate pests, diseases, and contamination. Our AI monitors environmental seals and alerts you to any potential breaches before they become issues.
